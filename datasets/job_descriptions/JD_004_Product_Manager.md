## Job Summary

We are seeking an experienced, user-obsessed **Product Manager** to own one of our core product verticals at NextGen Digital Labs. This is a high-visibility, high-impact individual contributor role where you will be responsible for the complete product lifecycle — from discovery and roadmapping to delivery, launch, and iteration. You will work at the intersection of user insights, business strategy, and engineering execution to build products that enterprise users love.

The ideal candidate has 3–5 years of PM experience in a B2B SaaS, fintech, or enterprise software environment, is deeply comfortable with data-driven decision-making, and has the communication skills to align engineering, design, sales, and C-suite stakeholders around a shared product vision.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Product Strategy & Roadmap:** Own the product roadmap for an assigned vertical; define 6-month and 12-month strategy aligned with company OKRs and market opportunity; get buy-in from leadership and cross-functional teams.
- **Discovery & Research:** Conduct continuous user interviews (minimum 8–10 per quarter), competitive analysis, and market research to validate problems before building solutions.
- **Requirement Definition:** Write detailed PRDs (Product Requirements Documents), user stories, acceptance criteria, and workflow mockups that engineering and design teams can execute against without ambiguity.
- **Backlog Management:** Prioritize and maintain a healthy, ranked product backlog using frameworks like RICE, MoSCoW, and Kano Model; ensure sprint readiness at all times.
- **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Act as the central coordination point between Engineering, Design, QA, Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, and Finance; lead sprint reviews, roadmap reviews, and quarterly business reviews (QBRs).
- **Metrics & Analytics:** Define success metrics for every feature launched; track adoption, engagement, churn, and revenue impact using Mixpanel, Amplitude, and internal BI tools; drive A/B experiments.
- **Go-to-Market (GTM):** Collaborate with Marketing and Sales to plan feature launches; create internal enablement materials (battle cards, FAQs, demo scripts) and external product communications.
- **Customer Engagement:** Participate in strategic customer calls and QBRs; gather feedback and translate enterprise customer needs into product requirements.
- **Stakeholder Management:** Present product strategy, metrics, and decisions to C-suite and board-level stakeholders; manage expectations around timelines and tradeoffs.
- **Process Improvement:** Drive continuous improvement in PM processes — PRD templates, review cadences, and product analytics frameworks across the PM team.
