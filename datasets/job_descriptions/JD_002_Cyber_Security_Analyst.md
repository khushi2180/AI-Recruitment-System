## Job Summary

SecureNet Technologies is actively seeking highly motivated **Cyber Security Analysts** to join our Tier-1 and Tier-2 SOC teams. In this role, you will be responsible for continuous monitoring, detection, triage, and response to security incidents across client environments. You will work with cutting-edge SIEM platforms, endpoint detection tools, and threat intelligence feeds to protect some of India's most critical enterprise infrastructure.

This is an excellent entry-level opportunity for cybersecurity graduates and professionals with 0–2 years of experience who wish to build a career in threat detection, incident response, or penetration testing. You will receive hands-on training, mentorship from L3/L4 analysts, and a clear promotion path to L2 within 12–18 months.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Security Monitoring:** Continuously monitor security alerts and events generated from SIEM platforms (Splunk, IBM QRadar, Microsoft Sentinel) across client environments during assigned shifts.
- **Alert Triage:** Investigate, prioritize, and classify security alerts based on severity; escalate high-priority incidents to L2/L3 teams within defined SLAs.
- **Incident Response:** Participate in the end-to-end incident response lifecycle — containment, eradication, recovery, and post-incident reporting using structured playbooks.
- **Threat Hunting:** Conduct proactive threat hunting exercises to identify indicators of compromise (IoCs) and attacker TTPs mapped to MITRE ATT&CK framework.
- **Vulnerability Assessment:** Assist in Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) engagements for clients using tools like Nessus, Nmap, Metasploit, and Burp Suite.
- **Log Analysis:** Analyze firewall logs, IDS/IPS alerts, endpoint detection logs, and network traffic captures to identify malicious activity.
- **Reporting:** Prepare daily shift reports, incident reports, and monthly security dashboards for client stakeholders and internal management.
- **Compliance Support:** Support clients with evidence collection and gap assessments for ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and NIST frameworks.
- **Tool Administration:** Assist in SIEM rule tuning, alert suppression, and dashboard creation to reduce false positive rates.
- **Knowledge Base:** Contribute to internal runbooks, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and threat intelligence repositories.
