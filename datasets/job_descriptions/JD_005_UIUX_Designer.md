## Job Summary

PixelCraft Studio is looking for a talented, portfolio-strong **UI/UX Designer** to join our core product design team. You will work on end-to-end product design challenges — from early-stage discovery and user research to high-fidelity prototypes and design system contributions. You will collaborate daily with PMs, developers, and content strategists, owning the design experience for multiple client projects simultaneously.

The ideal candidate is deeply empathetic towards users, obsessed with pixel-perfect visual quality, and comfortable presenting and defending design decisions using data and research. You are someone who thinks in systems, not just screens, and who sees design as a problem-solving discipline — not just a beautification exercise.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **UX Research & Discovery:** Conduct user interviews, contextual inquiries, heuristic evaluations, and usability tests to understand user needs and pain points; synthesize findings into actionable design insights.
- **Wireframing & IA:** Create low-fidelity wireframes, user flows, site maps, and information architecture diagrams for web and mobile applications.
- **Visual UI Design:** Design high-fidelity, pixel-perfect screens in Figma for web platforms (responsive design: desktop, tablet, mobile) and native mobile apps (iOS + Android guidelines).
- **Prototyping:** Build interactive prototypes in Figma with realistic micro-interactions, transitions, and animations for user testing and client presentations.
- **Design Systems:** Contribute to and maintain a shared design system (component library, tokens, pattern library) in Figma that scales across multiple products.
- **User Testing:** Plan and moderate usability testing sessions (moderated/unmoderated); analyze results and iterate on designs based on findings.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Work hand-in-hand with React/Flutter developers to ensure design-to-code fidelity; participate in design handoff using Figma's Dev Mode and Zeplin.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure all designs comply with WCAG 2.1 AA standards — color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility.
- **Client Presentations:** Present design concepts, rationale, and user research findings to clients and leadership; incorporate feedback through structured design review cycles.
- **Design QA:** Conduct design QA during development sprints to catch and document visual regressions and UX inconsistencies before releases.
