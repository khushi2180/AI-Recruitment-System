## Job Summary

We are looking for a sharp, curious, and detail-oriented **Data Analyst** to join our Analytics Delivery team in Hyderabad. In this role, you will work directly with business stakeholders and project managers to translate raw data into actionable insights that drive measurable business outcomes. From building dashboards and writing complex SQL queries to identifying trends and presenting findings to leadership, you will be at the center of data-driven decision-making at Insight Analytics.

You will be embedded in client project teams and expected to deliver high-quality analysis with minimal supervision. If you are passionate about turning messy data into clean stories and love the moment when a chart reveals something unexpected, this is the role for you.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Data Extraction & Transformation:** Extract, clean, and transform large structured and semi-structured datasets from multiple sources using SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy), and ETL tools.
- **Dashboard Development:** Build and maintain interactive, real-time dashboards and reports for business stakeholders using Tableau, Power BI, and Google Looker Studio.
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Conduct thorough EDA to identify trends, patterns, outliers, and anomalies; provide data-backed hypotheses to business teams.
- **KPI Monitoring:** Define, track, and report on key business metrics across sales, operations, marketing, and supply chain functions.
- **Statistical Analysis:** Apply descriptive and inferential statistics (regression, correlation, hypothesis testing) to support business forecasting and planning.
- **Data Modeling:** Design and maintain star/snowflake schema data models in data warehouses (Redshift, BigQuery, Snowflake).
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Present findings to non-technical business stakeholders using clear visualizations and storytelling; create executive-level presentation decks.
- **Data Quality Management:** Implement data validation checks, anomaly detection rules, and data governance practices across client pipelines.
- **Collaboration:** Work closely with Data Engineers, ML Engineers, and Business Analysts to deliver integrated analytics solutions.
- **Documentation:** Maintain clear documentation for data definitions, transformation logic, and analytical methodologies.
